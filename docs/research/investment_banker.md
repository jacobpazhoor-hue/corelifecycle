## The 8-level ladder + escalation spine (the numbers on screen)
1. **Analyst** (yrs 1–3) — base ~$110–150K + bonus → ~$150–200K all-in. 80–100h weeks, pitch books,
   DCFs, comps, "you are a function." Overlay: **$150,000**.
2. **Associate** (post-MBA / promoted) — ~$175–250K base, all-in ~$250–450K. Manages analysts,
   runs the model, on client calls but doesn't own them. Overlay: **$350,000**.
3. **Vice President** — all-in ~$450–700K. Runs deal execution day-to-day, manages the process and
   the client relationship's logistics. Overlay: **$600,000**.
4. **Director / SVP** — ~$700K–$1.2M. The pivot from *execution* to *origination* (rainmaking).
5. **Managing Director** — **$1–3M+**, mostly bonus tied to fees you bring in. You ARE the
   relationship; you eat what you kill. Overlay: **$2,000,000**.
6. **Group Head / Head of Investment Banking** — **$5M+**. Run the division, set which deals get
   resourced, own the league-table ranking. Overlay: **$6,000,000**.
7. **Buy-side power** — leave for PE/hedge fund; deploy capital, take board seats; **carry worth
   $40–90M** if the fund performs. You stop thinking in salary. Overlay: **$50,000,000**.
8. **The Architect** — sovereign wealth / sets allocation mandates; **when you move, markets move
   before the press release**. Carry in the **billions**, anonymous, the system itself.
   Overlay: **$2,400,000,000**. (mirror the series' "Architect" ending + the cyclical young analyst)

## Real mechanics to name (specificity = credibility)
pitch book, DCF, trading comps, league tables, the bulge bracket, IPO / book-building, M&A advisory,
the ~1–2% advisory fee on a multi-billion deal, leveraged buyout, high-yield bond issuance,
the P&L of the division, carried interest ("carry"), the 2-and-20, LP capital (sovereign funds,
pensions), the Bloomberg terminal, the 6 a.m. pitch, the bonus number that defines the year.

## Emotional arc (same hardening, finance flavor)
earnest/sleepless → first moral gray zone (a number you don't flag) → cold execution →
rainmaker (relationships = leverage) → running the machine → deploying capital on people you never
meet → anonymous market-mover who feels nothing when a position makes $380M in a week.
